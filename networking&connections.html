Networking & Connections

Building Your Professional Network

Networking is not just about asking for jobs—it’s about cultivating genuine relationships that support your growth. Connect with peers, alumni, and professionals to learn about roles, industries, and opportunities.

Strategies for Effective Networking

  1. Attend events: Career fairs, workshops, and talks provide face-to-face time with employers and alumni.
  2. Leverage LinkedIn: Build a complete profile, join industry groups, and reach out to alumni.
  3. Use informational interviews: Ask about career paths, work environments, and advice. The TIARA method makes this simple.
  4. Stay in touch: Send thank-yous, share updates, and nurture connections over time.

Tips for Students New to Networking

  • Start small: connect with classmates and instructors first.
  • Practice a 30-second intro about your interests and skills.
  • Set a monthly goal, like attending one event or messaging two alumni.
